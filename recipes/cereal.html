Cereal

Description

This cereal recipe is one of the best you'll ever have. Honey Bunches of Oats is an S tier cereal, guaranteed to make you happy.

Ingredients
  • Honey Bunches of Oats
  • Milk
Directions
  1. Place desired amount of cereal in your bowl - more is always better
  2. Pour milk over your cereal. NEVER pour milk before your cereal.
  3. Enjoy!
