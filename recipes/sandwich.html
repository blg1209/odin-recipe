Basic Sandwich

Description

I'm going to show you how to make one of the most boring and basic meals you can have, a sandwich! If you're strapped for cash this is for you. Broke Bois unite

Ingredients
  • White Bread
  • Turkey, Ham, Roast Beef, or whatever meat you have laying around
  • Mayonaise
  • Mustard
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Hot Cheetos
Instructions
  1. Get your bread - toast it if you're feeling fancy
  2. Put mayonaise on both sides, followed by mustard on only one side
  3. Get a ton of meat and slap it on that bread
  4. Place 1 - 2 slices of cheese on your bread. More cheese is always better
  5. Lastly pile a bunch of Hot Cheetos on your plate, followed by loading up that sandwich with Hot Cheetos
